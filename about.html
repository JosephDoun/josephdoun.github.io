About

Iosif, or __Joseph__, as he likes being called, is a __Machine Learning__ professional specialised in __GIS technologies__. But, first and foremost, he is an **Engineer** who enjoys planning and building projects from the ground up. He studied **Spatial Planning & Development Engineering** at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, from which he received his integrated **M.Eng.** in 2020. There, he learned research methodologies and how to deeply analyze and evaluate the living space in economical, social and environmental terms using **GIS technologies**, **Data Science** and **Machine Learning**. During the writing of his thesis he became increasingly interested in Machine Learning algorithms, programming and computer vision. As a result, upon graduation he invested his time in learning the ins and outs of various ML algorithms and studied Deep Learning through the lectures and paradigms of popular DL advocates such as Andrew Ng and Andrej Karpathy. His favorite Data Science lecturer, however, is Kilian Weinberger. In **2022** he became part of the RSLab team as a Research Assistant for 6 months, in Herakleion, Greece, where he learned first hand about large scale image processing and computational algorithms.
